### Quiche

## Ingredients
* 1 flaky pie crust
* 4 large eggs
* ½ cup of milk
* ½ cup of cream
* 1 cup of shredded cheese
* 2 cups of extras
* ¼ teaspoon salt
* ¼ teaspoon pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
2. Beat the eggs, whole milk, heavy cream, salt, and pepper together.
3. Add in 'extra' ingredients.
4. Bake in oven for 45-55 minutes.
